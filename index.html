How we do it

Basically, we read minds

Analyzing the prospect’s behavior, we hunt for their thinking patterns and conscious set of considerations, in order to gradually obtain a predictable algorithm comprising all the main rational factors that play a crucial role in his process of decision making.

The silent advisors

Trying to reach a deeper layer of the prospects’ set of considerations, we start mapping the prospects associative world, carefully unveiling the deeper motivation that unconsciously cast their effect on the prospect process of decision making. You could call it their intuition.
{Fr+Fe =D}

You believe yourself the most

As a final act we carefully compose a customized algorithm, containing an accurate composition of selected conscious and unconscious decision making stimulus, thus helping the prospect deduce the desired decisions by himself, and insuring a high probability for an intense and accurate reaction.

Fe = The ratio of compatibility between the emotional qualities of the brand and the prospect's emotional/associative/intuition aspirations Fr = The ratio of compatibility between the Rational characteristics of the brand and the prospect's rational demands D = The intensity of wanting in an individual from the target audience
